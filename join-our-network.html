Why Join Us

See what optometrists who have joined our network have to say about their experience and the benefits.

In 2017, I was actively looking to sell my practice after 32 years, but I was not ready to retire. When PHUNG EYE CARE, LLC (formerly TZVECL) purchased my practice, I was able to stay on as an employee with all the benefits, we kept the staff and even the name of the practice stayed the same. Over two years later, and it is still the best decision I have made in my optometric career.
Dr. Peter C. Dubin, Walterboro, SC
Joining PHUNG EYE CARE, LLC (formerly TZVECL) has allowed me to be able to focus solely on patient care without the stress of daily decision-making that comes with practice ownership. It has been great to be able to come to work with the same practice name, the same work family and be able to just see patients!

    Meet the PHUNG EYE CARE, LLC family.

    PHUNG EYE CARE, LLC is an integrated group of owned and managed optometry locations who are passionate about delivering the best outcomes for patients and our members. Our leadership team includes licensed optometrists and seasoned business experts with deep experience in the eyecare industry. Currently, we operate eyecare practices in North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Colorado, Minnesota, Iowa, and Tennessee, and are growing through the acquisition of practices who want to be part of a larger network.
